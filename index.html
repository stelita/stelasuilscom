<Here's a bit more about me/>

I am the co-founder of Diversitude, a Diversity, Inclusion & Belonging Consultancy. We provide customised solutions for companies and institutions, such as Gender Equality Plans (GEPs), DIB workframes, Unconscious Bias and Allyship at Work Training. Our goal is to build inclusive workplaces where women of all identities feel empowered and belonging by making of diversity an attitude.

I also direct the D&I area on CollectiveUp, a social enterprise that does research to create methods and technologies to achieve the SDG Goals. As a pro bono, I lead Lean In Portugal, an NGO that supports women achieve their ambitions.

I am a trained Psychologist and a PhD Candidate in Gender Studies. I focus on retention and promotion of women working in ICT. 
After 2 years consulting ThreeFold, a blockchain startup, I founded an agency to help tech companies recruit diverse talent, which pivoted to connect global talent with Dubai and Abu Dhabi based companies.

I'm passionate about building an inclusive Metaverse. In love with NFTs, minted World of Women #1027 and became an early mod at launch to give them a hand. I started breeding CryptoKitties in 2016 and I’m a proud owner of PawpStar, a Gen 0.

I facilitated a few Masterclasses at the Web Summit 2019 (and now getting ready for 2022), and Collision in (2020), as well as some other international conferences.

A new mom, I love having fun with my son, husband, and two doggies. In my free time, I catch some waves with my bodyboard on the coast of Portugal. Real Madrid fan. Peanut butter enthusiast.

You can also find me on Twitter and LinkedIn, read my latest article on Unconscious Bias in HR and fund my research - stela.eth
